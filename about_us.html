About Us

Making sense of logistics so you don’t have to

About us – Caravan Supply Chain is a brokerage, third-party logistics (3PL), and fourth-party logistics (4PL) service provider based in Burlington Ontario, Canada. We provide full-service, asset-based third-party logistics solutions anywhere in North America.
Caravan Supply Chain, a subsidiary of Caravan Group of Companies, are supply chain experts with a focus and commitment to solving complex logistics challenges for our valued clients.
Using a blend of company assets and extensive carrier network, we are able to bring capacity at the right cost to every solution. We’re making sense of logistics, so you don’t have to.
